If you want to spend a great time this summer in one of the best summer camps in the USA, boost your English and CV working as a camp counselor for American kids and travel to the beautiful destinations of the land of opportunity, then don't miss this opportunity.

Can I become a Camp Counselor?
Yes, you can if:

You are at least 18 years old

You are a university student

You have enough English skills

You are vaccinated against COVID-19

Benefits of the program:

Free accommodation and food in the camp

Free Transfer from the airport to the camp

Traveling around the US after the program

Competitive salary for two months

Beautiful nature and landscapes of the camp

Dynamic entertainment and sports activities present
